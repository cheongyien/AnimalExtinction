About Us

Due to the increase in human population, the demand for food as well as infrastructures also rises. In order to cope with this demand, more forests or animalsâ€™ habitats are removed for these purposes, which in turn contributes to the endangerment of certain animals. Apart from that, illegal hunting to sell animal parts for a high price has also negatively affected the issue.

Our website is created to spread knowledge regarding endangered species. In our website, several animals (Mammal, Reptile, Bird and Fish), which are facing extinction, will be introduced. In addition, the factors that cause the endangerment of the species will also be displayed.

Feel free to subscribe us if you are interested in knowing more animals which are endangered!
